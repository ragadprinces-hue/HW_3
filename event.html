About this event

The AI Research Expo brings together undergraduate and graduate students, instructors, and industry guests to present applied research projects. Attendees can explore poster sessions, interactive prototypes, and short talks focused on responsible AI, learning technologies, data visualization, and student-built tools.

The program is designed to be accessible for all students. You can join to learn about current research, discover collaboration opportunities, or simply get inspired by how technology is being used across academic disciplines.
