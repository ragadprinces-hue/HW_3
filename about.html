Platform description

Built for students, organizers, and campus communities.

Our platform helps students quickly find events that match their interests while giving organizers a clear, reusable structure for presenting event details.

The guide focuses on practical browsing, strong visual hierarchy, responsive cards, simple filters, and accessible content patterns that work in Arabic and English.

01

Easy discovery

Events are grouped into clear categories with concise details, direct actions, and friendly navigation.

02

Inclusive access

Bilingual content and RTL/LTR-ready layouts make the experience useful for a wider university audience.

03

Quality publishing

Every event should include accurate dates, location, category, description, and organizer contact path.

04

Mobile first

Cards, navigation, forms, and sections are designed to stay readable and comfortable on small screens.

Publishing policies

Simple rules for trustworthy event listings.

Policies keep the platform useful, safe, and consistent for the whole university community.

✓

Accurate details

Each event should include a correct title, date, time, location, category, and clear description before publishing.

✓

Respectful content

Listings must be inclusive, student-friendly, and aligned with university community standards.

✓

Timely updates

Organizers should update cancelled, postponed, or location-changed events as early as possible.

✓

Accessible communication

Descriptions should be concise, readable, and understandable in both Arabic and English where available.
